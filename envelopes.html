Envelopes

Envelopes help you organize your finances into specific budget categories. In Simple Envelope, there are two main types:

  • Spending Envelopes: Track day-to-day expenses and monitor your available funds.
  • Savings Envelopes: Manage your saving goals by recording deposits, withdrawals, and tracking progress toward your savings targets.
