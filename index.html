Your Guide to Simple Envelope

Learn about transactions, envelopes (spending and savings), and how to get the most out of your budgeting app.

Transactions

A transaction represents a single financial activity recorded within Simple Envelope. Whether it's an expense or a fund addition, each transaction records the date, amount, and a note describing the event.

Envelopes

Envelopes help you organize your finances by grouping money into specific budgets. There are two types:

  • Spending Envelopes: Track daily expenses and monitor your available funds.
  • Savings Envelopes: Designed for saving money, these envelopes not only track deposits and withdrawals but also include a goal amount to help you achieve your savings targets.

How to Use Simple Envelope

  1. Create an Envelope: Begin by creating an envelope with a name, color, and (optional) initial funds. Choose a spending or savings envelope based on your needs.
  2. Add Transactions: Record each financial event by adding transactions. Fill in the date, amount, and a brief note.
  3. Manage Your Budget: Review your envelopes to monitor your spending or savings progress. Edit envelopes and transactions as needed.
  4. Export Data: Easily export your transaction history as CSV for further review and record keeping.
