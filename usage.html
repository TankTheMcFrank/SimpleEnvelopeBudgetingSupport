How to Use Simple Envelope

  1. Create an Envelope: Start by creating an envelope with a name, color, and (optionally) initial funds. Choose a spending or savings envelope based on your needs.
  2. Add Transactions: Record every financial activity as a transaction by entering the date, amount, and a brief note.
  3. Monitor Your Budget: Review your envelopes to track spending or savings progress. Edit transactions and envelopes as needed.
  4. Export Data: Easily export your transaction history as a CSV file for record keeping or further analysis.
